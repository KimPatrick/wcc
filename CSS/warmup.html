Favorite Person in History

Grab the broom of anger and drive off the beast of fear. ~Zora Neale Hurston

Zora Neale Hurston was a novelist, short story writer, folklorist, and anthropologist. She is my favorite person in history because she believed that even though the odds were stacked against her, if she kept striving, she would be able to accomplish her goals.
