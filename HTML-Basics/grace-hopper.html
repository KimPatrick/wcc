Grace Hopper

[The Computer] was the first machine man built that assisted the power of his brain instead of the strength of his arm. - Grace Hopper

Computer programmer Grace Hopper helped develop a compiler that was a precursor to the widely used COBOL language and became a rear admiral in the U.S. Navy.

Interesting Facts

  1. Born in New York City in 1906
  2. Joined the U.S. Navy during World War II
  3. Was assigned to program the Mark I computer while in the U.S. Navy
  4. Led the team that created the first language compiler, which led to the popular COBOL language
